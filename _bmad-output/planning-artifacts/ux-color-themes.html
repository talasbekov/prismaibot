Design Rules
  • Quiet warmth must never reduce contrast below comfortable readability.
  • Terracotta is for calm emphasis, not gamified excitement.
  • Sage is best for continuity and supportive low-pressure states.
  • Alert red is restrained and should work with wording, not replace it.
  • Long dense reply blocks are an accessibility failure, even with a good palette.
